About us

At Griffin Grinding, we are committed to delivering high-quality mining solutions at competitive prices. With a focus on reliability, performance, and precision, we tailor our products to meet the unique needs of the industry.

Founded in 2000, our factories span over 15,000 square meters, supported by a dedicated team of 150 employees, including 30 research and development specialists. We pride ourselves on producing grinding media with exceptional hardness, wear resistance, and toughness.

Certified with ISO 9001:2008, our “quality first” philosophy drives everything we do, ensuring we consistently provide some of the best products in the industry.
